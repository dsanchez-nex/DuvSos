## Summary

This PR delivers two major OpenSpec changes:

### 1. `restructure-reminders-module` (archived)
- **Recurrence engine**: daily/weekly/monthly/annual with exceptions, lifecycle, pause/resume
- **Milestones**: creation, auto-suggestions, item grouping
- **Conflict detection**: cross-module conflict checking
- **Priority escalation**: background job for approaching deadlines
- **Calendar dashboard**: unified month view with workload overlay
- **Advanced reminder form**: recurrence, exceptions, blockers, lifecycle
- **9 new specs** synced to `openspec/specs/`

### 2. `restructure-dashboard` (archived)
- **2-column layout**: main content + right sidebar (inspired by old dashboard)
- **Habit Trackers** as first section (5-column grid, max 5 habits, prioritize incomplete)
- **Weekly Compliance** integrated below habit trackers
- **Checklists & ToDos** side-by-side (50/50), sorted by priority
- **Planning**: month calendar with workload intensity overlay
- **Scorecard** (streak, projects, tasks, compliance) in right sidebar
- **Upcoming Reminders** (functional, 14-day range) in right sidebar
- **5 new specs** synced to `openspec/specs/`

### Additional Changes
- Root `/` redirects to `/dashboard`
- Sidebar Dashboard link points to `/dashboard`
- Login redirect goes to `/dashboard`
- Old `DashboardContent`, `RightSidebar`, and `page.tsx` removed
- Retrofuturista theme fully migrated across all pages
